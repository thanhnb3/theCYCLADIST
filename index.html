ABOUT US
Sharing the soul of the Cyclades
Having lived, travelled and worked across the globe, Petros Stamatopoulos and Maira Tagalaki still daydream about their childhood summer holidays in the Cyclades. They find themselves returning as often as they can to enjoy all the unique experiences each island has to offer. Their passion for introducing friends from around the world to their favourite places-coupled with their understanding of luxury travel-led to the birth of The Cycladist.
